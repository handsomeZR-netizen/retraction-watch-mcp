A Lightweight Index Structure for Time-Series Anomaly Lookup

Diane Wong, Erik Larsson, Fatima Hassan

Department of Computer Science, Northbridge Institute of Technology, Singapore

Keywords: time series, indexing, anomaly detection

\section{Introduction}
We describe a memory-efficient indexing scheme for time-series anomaly
retrieval, with constant-time lookup for windowed queries.

\section{Method}
Our approach combines hashing with sketch-based summaries.

\section{Conclusions}
The proposed structure achieves sublinear lookup with bounded error.
